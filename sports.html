About the Siddiq Falcons

The Siddiq Falcons is the vibrant sports wing of Mahallah Siddiq, boasting a diverse array of sports teams, each dedicated to excellence, sportsmanship, and community spirit. From the football pitch to the basketball court and beyond, our teams represent the dynamic and united spirit of Mahallah Siddiq.

As the Home of the Falcons, Mahallah Siddiq takes pride in the achievements and spirit of the Siddiq Falcons, celebrating their victories, resilience in challenges, and the lasting impact they make both on and off the sports arena. The Siddiq Falcons truly embody the essence of sportsmanship, teamwork, and community, contributing to the vibrant tapestry of life within Mahallah Siddiq.

Since its establishment in 2001, the Falcons have soared through various sports arenas, building a reputation for resilience and determination. The founding members envisioned a dynamic and united sports community, and over the years, the Siddiq Falcons have evolved into a powerhouse of diverse sports teams.

Celebrating over two decades of achievements, Mahallah Siddiq remains committed to fostering a culture of athleticism, teamwork, and spirited competition.
