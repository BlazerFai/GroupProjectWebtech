7 Things To Know In Mahallah

1. Check in & Check out room

  • Compulsory to check in at the beginning of the semester and check out at the end of the semester
  • Vacate & clean the room and surrender the room key at the end of every semester

2. Live In During Vacation (LIDV)

  • Application for group/official LIDV is to be submitted to RSD/mahallah office within the stated date
  • LIDV for personal reasons and late requests of LIDV are considered as rental
  • Students who fail to apply LIDV and vacate the room and surrender the room key at the end of the semester will be given a compound and will be charged for LIDV

3. Vacation Notice (Available in I-Maalum, Fb & RSD Website)

4. Mahallah Online Room Registration (MORR)

  • All students who will register for the coming semester are required to do MORR at the stated date in vacation notice (2 weeks before the final exam)
  • Failure to do MORR may cause students to have no room for the next semester, and students have to vacate their room and surrender the room key
  • Students who are not eligible to do MORR due to various reasons need to fill in details in the google form (bit.ly/morr_sem1 or scan QR code)

HOW TO GET ROOM FOR NEXT SEM: Mahallah Online Room Registration (MORR) – (Available in I-Maalum, FB & RSD Website)

5. Live Off Campus (LOC)

Students who wish/have to stay off-campus may register themselves at Mahallah Office.
