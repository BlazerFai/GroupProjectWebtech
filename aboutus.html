ABOUT US

This Mahallah was established in 1997. With an approximate area of 6.5 acres, this Mahallah has 7 residential blocks that can cater up to 940 residents and 1 central facilities block. This Mahallah was named to commemorate Sayyidina Abu Bakr as-Siddiq RA, his towering personality and his brilliant deeds to Islam and the Muslim Ummah. This Mahallah, like other 15 Mahallat in IIUM Gombak Campus, is under the joint care of Residential and Services Department and Student Affairs and Development Division.
